# Getting Started

## Introduction

Welcome to the **UmbraMix** documentation! UmbraMix is a cutting-edge privacy solution designed to bring financial anonymity to the Starknet ecosystem. By leveraging the power of zero-knowledge proofs, the Lightning Network, and Cashu ecash, UmbraMix ensures that your transactions remain private, secure, and untraceable.

Whether you are a developer looking to integrate privacy features into your dApp or a user seeking financial sovereignty, this documentation will guide you through the core concepts, architecture, and usage of UmbraMix.

### Why Privacy Matters?

In the world of public blockchains, every transaction is visible to everyone. While transparency is a core feature of blockchain technology, it also exposes users to surveillance, data mining, and potential security risks. UmbraMix restores the balance by providing optional privacy for those who need it.

## Quick Start

Ready to start mixing? Follow these simple steps to make your first private transaction on Starknet.

### Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

1.  **Starknet Wallet**: Install a compatible wallet like [Argent X](https://www.argent.xyz/argent-x/) or [Braavos](https://braavos.app/).
2.  **Funds**: ensure you have some ETH or STRK in your wallet to cover gas fees and the transaction amount.
3.  **Browser**: Use a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Brave.

### Step-by-Step Guide

1.  **Connect Your Wallet**:
    - Navigate to the [UmbraMix Dashboard](/dashboard).
    - Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right corner.
    - Approve the connection in your wallet pop-up.

2.  **Choose Your Mix Mode**:
    - **Full Mix**: Automated process where funds are deposited and sent to a recipient address of your choice.
    - **Split Mix**: More control. You receive Cashu tokens which you can redeem later or send to others.

3.  **Initiate a Mix**:
    - Enter the amount you wish to mix.
    - (Optional) Set a recipient address if different from your connected wallet.
    - Click "Mix Now" and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

4.  **Wait for Completion**:
    - The process involves several steps (Deposit -> Lightning Swap -> Cashu -> Withdrawal).
    - You can track the progress in the "History" tab.
